Rules And Regulations

Students and their parents are expected to go through the following Rules and regulations carefully, as School and Hostel activities are governed by these.

  • Hostel Rules

  • Students should keep their beds, cupboards, books etc. neat, clean and in proper order.
  • Students are not allowed to have expensive things like gold rings or any other ornaments. They should not keep money with them. Pocket money should be deposited with the Warden or Tutor.
  • A built-in cupboard with a lockable shelf is provided. This has a Godrej lock and 3 keys.
    If a key is lost, the student who has lost it will have to pay for its replacement.
  • Parents should not send money to their wards through Money Orders. They can send money only through bank drafts.
  • Students should not have suitcases larger than 28” x 22” x 19” (Length 28”, Width 22” & Height 19”) so that these can be place in the dormitory cupboards.

  • Library Rules

  • Students should maintain silence.
  • They should not move from seat to seat.
  • Any book borrowed from the library should be returned within a fortnight. Otherwise, a fine will be levied.
  • If a book is damaged or lost, the student involved will have to make good the loss or pay for the damage.
  • Students should not borrow library books from each other.

  • LEAVE OF ABSENCE

  • In an emergency, such as serious illness or death of a close relative, students are given permission by the principal.
  • Students should not be absent without obtaining prior permission from the principal.
  • They should report at School after the holidays on the appointed day.
  • If a student is sick or injured, the leave letter must be signed by the Staff Nurse and the Tutor-in- Charge.

  • Withdrawal

  • A Transfer Certificate will be issued only after the parent/ guardian has paid all the dues to the school and has applied for it in writing one week beforehand.

  • Dining Hall Rules

  • Students should follow the mess timings and observe table manners.
  • Food, Paper or any other item should not be thrown around.
  • Students should not shout to one another but should converse in a low voice.
  • Carrying foodstuffs out of the dining hall is prohibited.
  • Kitchen and Storeroom are out of bounds. Only those on dining-hall duty can enter the kitchen.
  • If a student causes damage to School or hostel property, including laboratory and other equipment, he/she will have to pay for repairs or replacement

  • NUTRITIOUS FOOD

  • The opulent eating place is something in which Seyad School takes very pleasure. The menu at the school has been carefully planned with the assistance of a registered nutritionist to provide students with a variety of proteins, grains, fruits and vegetables each day, all served in delicious vegetarian cuisine. Non vegetarian foods offer three meals every week.
