School Location

The School is located on a 35acre campus at the foot of the Podhigai Hills of the Western Ghats in a serene atmosphere. It is very near the Five Falls on Ilanji Road, Courtallam, about 8 kms. from Tenkasi town.
The faculty members are residing on the campus to guide the students at every step. It is our fervent hope that students, over the years, will develop the self-assurance and confidence needed to face the challenges of modern living.

The Seyad School on the outskirts of Courtallam is the first of its kind in the southernmost part of Tamil Nadu offering a unique spectrum of exceptional opportunities for study, interactive learning, experimentation and outbound activities. The academic and cultural ambience of the residential system in the School will expose the students to a wide range of activities. Their interest in sports, cultural activities and arts is awakened through encouragement and constant interaction. The School is Co-educational. Both boys and girls from nearby places can join the School as Day Scholars. There are separate Hostels for boys and girls.
